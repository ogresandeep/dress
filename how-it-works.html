HOW IT WORKS

This is how you can hire a dress from The Only Dress.

SEARCH

Browse our collection here on our website or on Instagram.

AVAILABILITY

Once you have decided on an item, select your dress size and event date to check its availability.

RENT

Once you have confirmation about the availability of your chosen dress for your event date, book it online.

WEAR

Wear your chosen designer dress for your event and look amazing!

RETURN

Return your dress to us in Manly or in an express post return envelope provided by The Only Dress. We will take care of the cleaning!

1. Search & Enquire About A Dress For Your Event

Browse our collection on our website or on Instagram. Once you have decided on an item, select your dress size and event date to check its availability. If not available, please submit a booking enquiry.


2. Book Your Dress

Once you have confirmation about the availability of your chosen dress for your event date, you can book it online.

The hire fee is due when you place your order and the $20 cost of cleaning the gown will be added to your order.

The garment is yours for the weekend or agreed time period.
Extended and holiday hires are available upon request.

Total costs to hire with us are:

The hire price as stated on website
+ $20 cleaning fee
+ $15 one-way postage OR $20 return postage // pick up is free!


3. Collect Your Dress

Pick up your dress from our store Manly - Sydney, as arranged with you. This is a free service.

Alternatively, we deliver dresses Australia-wide using Australia Post tracked Express service. Return shipping charge is $20.

If you choose to pick up the dress but return it by post, please let us know. Garment Collection - Pickup Only & Return by Postage is $15 and vice versa. If you want the dress delivered by post and then wish to drop it off in manly this one-way shipping costs $15.


4. Wear & Then Return Your Dress

After your event, returning your dress is simple.

If you have selected to pickup and/or drop in Manly, we will provide you with our location upon you making a booking.

If you have not selected to drop off the dress off at our Manly location, please place the dress in the provided prepaid Australia post satchel and send the parcel from your nearest Australia post office before 12-noon the day after your event. We track it from there.

There is no need to clean the dress; we take of everything.




For more information about our hire service please read our FAQs before making a booking.
