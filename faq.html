FAQ - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. Does TheOnlyDress have a physical shop?

No, we do not have a physical store/ shop. We are purely an online designer dress hire service. We don’t pay shop rent or fancy overheads, which means that we can pass valuable savings on to you! We provide a pick up option from our office space in Manly, Sydney for our local customers, as it would be silly to spend money on postage when you live around the corner.

2. Does TheOnlyDress offer a dress try-on service?

We do not offer a try-on service. Being an online only boutique we have no facilities to allow for our customers to try on a dress. Please note that we no longer offer postal try-on service as some girls unfortunately took advantage of this in the past. Our items are in high demand and often booked or with our cleaners before the next hire period starts.

3. Does TheOnlyDress offer a backup dress option for hires?

We offer all our customers the option to book and pay for 2 items and return the unworn garment for a store credit of the hire fee, which can be used towards renting another dress at another time. The unwanted and unworn dress must be returned on the day after receipt of both items via express post. Customers can purchase the extra-prepaid return postage bag from us for $15 AUD. All tags must be intact upon return.

4. What dress sizes do you stock?

We offer our garments in a variety of different sizes, which range in size from Australian size 4 – 14. Our size information is based on the label on the garment or the designers sizing chart and is a recommendation only. Please take care when choosing a garment that you are certain it will fit you. We endeavor to be as specific as possible describing the size and fit of each garments to ensure you are choosing an item that will fit and look good on you. We cannot offer a refund if an item does not fit. We encourage you to try on the dress or a similar style at the designer’s store, David Jones, Myer or other boutique that stocks the same garment. Please note, that Camilla items often fit differently even if it's the same style in the same size. The fit of an item may change during its lifetime. Fabrics may stretch from the very first wear.

If we are notified shortly after you receive the garment that you’d like to exchange the same garment for a different size then we will happily exchange it for you. Please do not hesitate reaching out to liaise. We will not rest until you have the best outfit for your event!

Camilla sizing:
1-3 (1 is the smallest and 3 is the largest size)
XS - XL (XS is the smallest and XL the largest size)
8 - 12 (8 is the smallest and 12 the largest size)
Camilla does not offer size 0 or 6.

Zimmermann sizing:
0P-3 (0P is the smallest and 3 is the largest size); only few Zimmermann styles include size 0P (6 petite)

Kookai sizing:
34 - 40 (34 is the smallest and 40 is the largest size)

5. Does the dress arrive professionally cleaned?

Yes! We are very proud of our high hygiene and cleaning standards. Every return is directly posted to our cleaners PO BOX and is cleaned as soon as it is received. Even garments that have only been tried on will be cleaned to ensure there is no makeup or odors on the item. Please note, some stains are unfortunately irremovable on certain fabrics. Hollywood tape residues may sometimes not come entirely off for example – so we ask that you please refrain from using this when wearing our garments.

6. Does the dress arrive wrinkle-free?

We do our best to ensure that every dress arrives to you wrinkle-free. After being steamed, all dresses are wrapped in tissue paper and placed in a waterproof satchel for protection. Certain fabrics are more prone to wrinkles, and if you receive a dress with slight creases, please try steaming the garment on low heat. Alternatively, you can hang the dress in a steamy bathroom and the steam will decrease any wrinkles. When steaming in a bathroom, please be sure to hang the dress where it won’t accidentally get splashed by the shower. Note: please do not iron the dress as this may cause permanent damage.

7. How long can I hire a dress?

Our standard rental period is over the weekend (the garment will be received by Friday). You either drop it off in person on Monday or lodge it at the post office on Monday before 12pm midday. If your hire date is during the week, the item must be returned on the next business day after the event.

EXTENDED HIRE IS NOW AVAILABLE FOR ONLY $50 MORE – This is for rental of garments longer than a weekend or for one event date (i.e. you may want to take it away on holidays (This covers you for having the garment for 2 hire periods).

8. What payment methods does TheOnlyDress accept for hiring dresses?

We offer the following payment options to complete your booking:

  • Credit/ Debit card (Visa and Mastercard)
  • ZipPay
  • AfterPay
9. The price stated on Instagram differs to the cost on the website. Which one do I have to pay?

Our prices may change from time to time as we reduce the price of older garments, and on the odd occasion we may increase the price on the most popular and often always booked out garments. Prices can go up or down. They are amended directly online and will be changed after the promotion finishes. The price stated on our website is always the current and correct price.

10. What's the deal with items on sale?

When a garment is a few seasons old and has some general wear and tear it will be put on sale. Sale items are not ‘for sale’ in the sense that you will own it out right, but rather it simply means the price to hire is reduced (on sale) from it’s original hire price. In case an item has very strong wear and tear we will mention this in the product description, however general wear and tear is to be expected when a garments hire fee is reduced (‘on sale’). If you are unsure about the condition of the specific item/ size you are enquiring for, please do not hesitate to contact us to ask. The condition of a garment can change with every wear (customer hire).

11. How do I know if an item is available?

We will do everything on our end to ensure that our website is kept updated with all the available stock we have on hand at all times. However we encourage you to SUBMIT A BOOKING ENQUIRY BEFORE PLACING AN ORDER.

Upon submitting a booking enquiry you will be notified of the garments availability for your event date(s) and further booking information via email. We keep items on hold for you for a short period of time, as stipulated in the email, which will give you sufficient time to place your order online.

12. Why do I have to provide follow up information to TheOnlyDress?

We require you to please confirm your postal address via SMS before we will dispatch your hired garment. No item will be posted without confirmation via your mobile phone. This is to double check you entered your postal address correctly and ensures that you will receive your order at the correct address.

For anyone coming to pick up a garment from our location in Manly, we require that you send us a photo of your personal ID (front and back) via SMS prior to coming to our office.

We also require a photo of your personal ID and bankcard of anyone that hires garments that have retail value of $1500AUD RRP or higher. This is in replace of us requesting you pay an insurance deposit.

13. Do I have to pay a security bond?

As of April 2018 we no longer required our customers to pay a bond when they hire from us. When you place an order online and agree to our terms of service (T&Cs) you give us permission to charge your bankcard in case of loss or damage of the item.

14. Why do I have to pay the $20 cleaning fee?

All our items are returned directly to our cleaners after hire. Every item, even those only tried on or worn once, gets cleaned and goes through our meticulous quality control and repairs process upon return. We are proud of our high cleaning and garment preservation standards at TheOnlyDress. Please do not attempt to clean the garment yourself under any circumstances. Depending on the condition in which a garment is returned to us, the cleaning costs us up to $45 per item. But we only charge you a flat rate of $20 AUD. Our hire prices stated on website do not include the cost of hand cleaning and any repairs (if necessary) to the garment after use. The cleaning fee is added to each garment you hire upon checkout.

15. What is the total cost for hiring a garment from TheOnlyDress?

The hire price as stated on website + $20 cleaning fee + $20 return express postage /or $15 one-way return postage for customers that picked up the garment by elect to post it back to us. Note: Pick up is free! There are no other hidden costs.

16. If I want to get my dress today, can I?

We do not work with same day postal couriers. However, you are welcome to pick up your dress from our office in Manly, Sydney on the same day as placing an order. Anyone can pick up on your behalf; this can be your own courier at your own cost. All pickups must be before 5pm and communicated and coordinated with us.

17. How long will it take for my dress to be delivered to me?

The estimated standard delivery time for express post is 1-2 business days - depending on your location and the time you submitted your order. Upon receiving a customers hire order, we post the dress out to you so that it will arrive one day prior to your event or to arrive on the day of event. This is usually a Thursday or Friday for weekend events. It is upon our discretion to post the garment earlier. Please allow 3-4 days for arrival if you live in a rural location and if in Western Australia. PLEASE NOTE: it is the customer’s responsibility to calculate how long the dress may take to arrive to your delivery address, and ensure that you place your order with sufficient time to ensure that the garment will arrive in time for your event.

18. Do you deliver Australia wide?

Yes, we deliver all around Australia to confirmed residential and business addresses, and PO Boxes.

19. Do I have to sign for my order?

Anyone present at the address at the time of delivery can sign on your behalf. If Australia Post attempted to deliver the parcel unsuccessfully, your parcel will be redirected to your nearest post office for collection.

20. Is there a shipping fee?

At TheOnlyDress we like to make things as simple as possible for you, so we offer a flat rate shipping fee for returning express post shipping. For only $20 you get two express postage bags. One containing your garment and one for the return of your garment to our office in Manly, Sydney. PLEASE NOTE: You must lodge your garment for return at a registered Australian Post office. The parcel cannot be dropped into a post box. Under no circumstances is the use of a red street box allowed.

21. Do you deliver on weekends?

Unfortunately we do not make deliveries or allow garment pick-ups on the weekend.

22. How do I cancel my order?

To cancel your order, please contact our Customer Service. Upon cancellation of your order, we will authorize a refund issued as store credit to be put towards another dress hire at another time. The shipping and cleaning fee will be kept as part of the store credit. PLEASE NOTE: We only issue store credits if your order is cancelled 7 business days prior to the beginning of your rental period . If you contact us after we have dispatched the garment, your rental period has started and we will not issue a cancellation of your order. Upon your cancellation request, if you fail to provide us with the requested and required personal documents we reserve the right not to issue you with a refund in the form of a store credit.

23. What if the dress doesn’t fit me?

It is your responsibility to make every effort to try on the dress or a similar dress by the same designer at their store or in David Jones or Myer prior to placing a hire booking with TheOnlyDress. Please take care when choosing and hiring a garment from us that you are confident that it will fit you. We endeavor to be as specific as possible describing the size and fit of all our garments on our website to ensure that you have enough information to know if it will fit you when choosing an item. Please note, that Camilla items often fit differently even if it is the same style in the same size! We will not issue any refunds if an item does not fit you. However, if time and availability permits, we may offer to exchange the size in the same dress or offer you another item for exchange. If you are able to please notify us on the day of receipt of the garment we will be happy to help you as best we can.

24. What if I don't end up wearing the garment I hired?

If you choose not to wear the garment you hired from us at your event, and do not directly notify us as soon as receiving it in the post, we unfortunately cannot provide you with a store credit. Upon receiving delivery of the dress, if you notify us that you are unable to wear the dress at your event, we will work with you to try and find a solution for you.

25. How do I return my dress?

Returning your dress is quick and easy! After your event, simply place the garment in the provided prepaid return express postage satchel that we provide to you with delivery of your garment, and lodge it at your nearest Post Office on the day after your event or on Monday before 12noon. PLEASE NOTE: You must lodge your garment for return at a registered Australian Post office and sign for it. The parcel cannot be dropped into a post box. Under no circumstances is the use of a red street box allowed.

26. What if I accidentally damage the dress?

We understand that some wear and tear may be inevitable when wearing the dress you hire. Please contact us if you’ve accidentally stained or damaged the dress so that we can prepare for its return and special cleaning/ repair. If the damage to the dress is significant you may be asked to pay for any alteration costs, costs associated with the replacement of the garment and/or any lost of business we have incurred due to the garment no longer being available for rehire. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions for more information.

27. What if the dress is lost or damaged beyond repair?

If the dress you hire from us gets lost, stolen or damaged beyond repair you will be liable to pay 150% of the retail price of the item as listed on our website or replace it with the same item in immaculate condition within 7 days. In addition to providing us with a replacement garment, you will be liable to pay us any incurred lost of business, in cases where the garment has already been booked for the following week. We know that this is a rare and unfortunate event, rest assured, we will work together with you to fid the best solution.

28. Does TheOnlyDress have any Terms & Conditions?

Yes we do. You can read our Terms and Conditions here. Many of the answers to these FAQ provide the basis of our T&C.
