BOOKING ENQUIRY

Here’s how to hire a dress from The Only Dress.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

  • We offer Australia wide Express Post shipping for a flat shipping cost of $20AUD.
  • Free garment pick-up and drop-off at our office in Manly, Sydney is also available upon request.
  • One-way return shipping is available for those that pick-up but wish to return the garment by post at the cost of $15AUD.
  • We do not offer international shipping (outside of Australia) at this point in time.
  • We look after all the garment cleaning and repairs. Please do not wash the garment after use. All our garments incur a non-refundable $20AUD compulsory cleaning fee upon checkout.
  • We do not have a show room or store in which you can come and try on a dress. It is your responsibility to ensure that the dress you book fits you. We encourage you to visit the designer’s boutique or another stockiest (David Jones, Myer etc.) to try on the dress prior to making a booking.
