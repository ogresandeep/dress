In cases where the dress has not been returned to TheOnlyDress by the Rental Return Due Date then TheOnlyDress shall be entitled to charge additional hire fees until the Dress is returned to TheOnlyDress and the Customer expressly authorizes TheOnlyDress to deduct such additional hire fees from the Customer’s credit card which was provided at the time of placement of the Customer’s order. Otherwise the hired garment is to be returned on the day after event. Garment must be returned from the same state that it had been delivered to (unless otherwise agreed).

Shipping Policy

TheOnlyDress’s standard delivery method is Express Signature postage operated by Australia Post. The estimated standard delivery time for express post is 1-2 business days - depending on your location and the time you submitted your order. Upon receiving a customers order, we post the dress out to you so that it will arrive one day prior to your event or to arrive on the day of event. This is usually a Thursday or Friday for weekend events. It is upon our discretion to post the garment earlier. Please allow 3-4 days for arrival if you live in a rural location and if in Western Australia. It is the customer’s responsibility to calculate how long the dress may take to arrive to your delivery address, and ensure that you place your order with sufficient time to ensure that the garment will arrive in time for your event. Customer bears the risk of delivery. The earliest we can possibly ship out a garment is on the next business day for orders placed by 6pm the previous day. Pick up must be liaised prior placing an order.
